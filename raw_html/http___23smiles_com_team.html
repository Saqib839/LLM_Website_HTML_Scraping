Advanced Dental Services

Preventive care is, of course, the mainstay of any good dental practice. Through regular exams and cleanings, Dr. Young and our staff want to keep you from experiencing any oral health issues. Through restorative care, Dr. Young can repair decayed, cracked, and otherwise damaged teeth. Our restorations include fillings, crowns, and root canal therapy. We are especially excited to prepare same-day crowns for qualifying patients. With this option, Dr. Young can prep your tooth and attach a restoration in just one appointment. Plus, same-day crowns are made in the office with strong, high-grade dental porcelain that mimics the translucency of natural tooth enamel.
