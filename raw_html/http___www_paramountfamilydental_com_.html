Meet Dr. John Hisel

Dr. Hisel and his wife Terri are high school sweethearts and have been married over 45 years. They both graduated from Boise High School in 1978. Dr. Hisel attended the College of Idaho graduating in 1982, Creighton University School of Dentistry graduating in 1986 and completed a United States Air Force General Practice Residency in 1987. After 2 years of General Duty in the USAF was complete, they chose Boise as their home, starting Private Practice in 1989. Together they have 2 married children and 6 grandchildren.

Meet Dr. Eric Hoidal

Dr. Hoidal was a member of the Deans list at Creighton and was also listed in Whos Who Among Students in Colleges and Universities. While in dental school, he was privileged to be a part of a medical/dental mission team traveling to the Dominican Republic. After dental school, Dr. Hoidal was accepted into the Idaho State University Advanced Education in General Dentistry Residency Program in Boise. He completed his residency on June 30, 2008. Professionally, Dr. Hoidal is a member of the American Dental Association, the Idaho State Dental Association and the Southwest Idaho Dental Society.
