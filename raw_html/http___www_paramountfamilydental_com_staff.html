Meet Our Doctors

Dr. John Hisel

Dr. Hisel was raised in the Boise area after moving with his family from California at age 4. He attended Boise schools graduating in 1978 from Boise High School along with his high school sweetheart, Terri. They were married shortly after high school and celebrate 46 years of marriage in March 2025! Together they have 2 married children and 6 grandchildren. Dr. Hisel and Terri make their home in Kuna along with a blind Springer Spaniel named Pippa and 2 horses.

Immediately after high school, Dr. Hisel attended College of Idaho graduating Cum Laude in 1982. It was during these college years they welcomed son, John III into their family.  Dr. Hisel was accepted into the pilot program of the Idaho Dental Education Program and he and Terri moved to Pocatello, Idaho for his first year of dental school.

9 months later they moved to Omaha, Nebraska where Dr. Hisel would attend Creighton’s Boyne School of Dental Science, graduating Suma Cum Laude in 1986. It was also during this year they welcomed daughter, Melissa into their family.  Dr. Hisel became a Captain in the United States Air Force in Fall of 1985, completing his General Practice Residency Training at Offutt Air Force Base Nebraska. After 2 years of General Duty at Fairchild Air Force Base, WA, Dr. Hisel and Terri set up Private Practice in Boise in August 1989.

Their son, John E Hisel, III is the Lead Pastor at Engage Boise and is married to Chandra who is a CPA in Meridian. John and Chandra have 4 children: Kristina, John IV,  Luke and Hope. 

Their daughter Melissa is a Pediatric Registered Dietitian and is a Principal Medical Liaison at Baxter Healthcare Corporation.  Melissa is married to Kent, who is a Physical Therapist employed at St. Luke’s Rehabilitation Hospital. Together they have 2 girls: Ainsley and Kinzlee.

Professional Info

Professionally, Dr. Hisel is active in numerous dental organizations including the American Dental Association, the Academy of General Dentistry, the Christian Medical and Dental Association, the Idaho State Dental Association, and the Southwest Idaho Dental Society. Dr. Hisel is also a member of the International College of Dentists-USA Section, and the Pierre Fauchard Academy for Dentists – both of which are dental organizations honoring Dentists for their continued commitment to Dentistry and service to their community. Dr. Hisel served the Idaho State Dental Society as its President in 2016-2017 and was awarded the ISDA’s President’s Award in 2018. In June 2021 Dr. Hisel was honored to receive the ISDA’s Lifetime Achievement Award.  Dr. Hisel has served as one of Idaho’s Delegates to the American Dental Association’s House of Delegates, as a member of the ISDA’s Legislative Committee and also has served our state as a consultant to the Idaho State Board of Dentistry. Dr. Hisel represented Idaho, Montana, Alaska, Oregon, and Washington on the Council of Government Affairs for the American Dental Association, and currently serves as a member of the Board of Trustees for the American Dental Association.

Volunteer Work

When he is not in our office, Dr. Hisel travels for the American Dental Association or can be found volunteering in any number of dental related community projects. He has served as a team Dentist for the Idaho Steelheads, was a member of the Idaho Dental Forensics Team and was on staff at St. Alphonsus Regional Medical Center for 25 years. Together with daughter Melissa, he serves on the Board of Directors for Idaho’s Fellowship of Christian Athletes. Dr. Hisel and his wife Terri have always been a great team and together they developed and continue to be a part of a free dental clinic in Garden City which exists today as Genesis Community Health.

Dr. Hisel and Terri are longtime members of CityHope Church in Boise and also attend son John’s church, Engage Boise. When they are not traveling, he and Terri enjoy spending time at their lakeside cabin in Donnelly, cycling, and spending time with their families. Dr. Hisel loves college football (Go Broncos!) loves to golf and runs to train for marathons and half marathons.

Dr. Eric Hoidal

Dr. Eric Hoidal has been with Paramount Family Dental since July 2008. He earned his undergraduate degree in Chemistry from the University of Oregon in 2003. While at Oregon, Dr. Hoidal was a member of the Dean’s list and received the 2003 Analytical Chemistry Award. He was also an active member of Campus Crusade for Christ. Dr. Hoidal was accepted into the Idaho Dental Education Program and attended his first year of dental school at Idaho State University. He then moved to Omaha, Nebraska to finish his final three years at Creighton University’s Boyne School of Dental Science.

Dr. Hoidal was a member of the Dean’s list at Creighton and was also listed in Who’s Who Among Students in Colleges and Universities. While in dental school, he was privileged to be a part of a medical/dental mission team traveling to the Dominican Republic. After dental school, Dr. Hoidal was accepted into the Idaho State University Advanced Education in General Dentistry Residency Program in Boise. He completed his residency on June 30, 2008. Professionally, Dr. Hoidal is a member of the American Dental Association, the Idaho State Dental Association and the Southwest Idaho Dental Society.

Personal Info

Dr. Hoidal was born and raised in Boise and attended Boise Schools. He graduated from Borah High School in 1999. While in high school, Dr. Hoidal participated in varsity football, basketball, track and tennis. He and his wife Michelle were married in the fall of 2009. They have 3 girls. Dr. Hoidal and his family live in Southwest Boise where they share their home with their dog, Griffin. They enjoy being outdoors and spending time as a family. His girls love to stop by the office to say hello. If you hear a little voice yelling “Daddy!” you know it is them looking for their favorite dentist.

Dr. Daniel Ackerman

Dr. Daniel Ackerman joined Paramount Family Dental in November 2023 meeting many of our patients while in the office 1-2 days per week. It didn’t take us long to figure out that he was “great fit” for our team members and our patients, and we were thrilled to have him join our practice full time in July 2024. Prior to joining our team, Dr. Ackerman spent 5 years practicing with his father in our valley. Dr. Ackerman is grateful to be a part of our amazing team and feels very much at home at Paramount Family Dental!

Dr. Ackerman graduated with honors from Northern Arizona University with a degree in Biochemistry before attending Oregon Health and Science University School of Dentistry where he also graduated with honors. While at OHSU, he participated in a selective Tucker Gold study club that focused on conservative esthetic treatment in gold, receiving recognition for outstanding achievement in Implant Dentistry and Periodontology.


Dr. Ackerman was raised in the Meridian area from the age of 3 and is thankful to be able to raise his family in the Treasure Valley. He and his wife, Danielle, are high school sweethearts and were married in 2015. They live in Boise with their 2 young daughters Amelia and Adelyn, as well as their golden doodle Sampson and their cat Peanut. When he’s not taking care of patients, Dr. Ackerman naturally stays busy with his young family and enjoys snowboarding, mountain biking, and snow shoeing. He and Danielle both love the outdoors and enjoy spending time camping and hiking together.


Professionally, Dr. Ackerman is a member of the Southwest Idaho Dental Society, the Idaho State Dental Association and the American Dental Association

A Personal Message From Dr Ackerman

“I am so excited to get to know the patients of this wonderful practice! It is my goal to earn your trust and care for all of your dental needs as well as build relationships on a personal level. Thank you for giving me an opportunity to get to know you, and I look forward to helping you maintain your oral health in a way that is tailored to your specific goals.”
